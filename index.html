Rows, a simple website structure.

Its goal is to center your content in the browser window while allowing you to extend background colors, borders and images to the edge of the page.

The .row div extends to the edge of the browser window, and the .row-inner div centers horizontally within the browser window.

Add background colors, images and styles to the .row div.

Add your main content to the .row-inner div.

If you do not want your page to extend to the edge of the browser, you can wrap your content in a .container div and define its width.

Stack rows down the page and do whatever you like inside them.

Enjoy.
